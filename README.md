## 👨‍💼 About Me

I'm a Senior Cloud DevOps Engineer with experience in building, scaling, and maintaining production systems for high-traffic applications. My journey in DevOps started with a passion for solving operational problems and has evolved into a deep expertise in cloud architecture, infrastructure automation, and reliability engineering. I believe that great DevOps is invisible—it's the backbone that lets engineering teams focus on shipping features without worrying about system stability or unexpected outages.

I also maintain an active blog on [Medium](https://ravijkyada.medium.com/) where I write about real-world DevOps challenges, AWS optimization strategies, Kubernetes best practices, and CI/CD patterns. I love sharing knowledge and learning from the broader Cloud community.
